Thank you for using Variety Wallpaper Changer.

If this is the first time you are publishing from Variety, you may have to configure your browser so that it opens variety:// links with Variety.

Firefox will ask you to choose an application, please choose /opt/extras.ubuntu.com/variety/bin/variety or /usr/bin/variety - depending on where Variety is installed on your machine.
Make sure to check the option Remember my choice for variety links so you don't have to do this next time.

Chrome/Chromium will ask you to open the link with xdg-open, which would itself launch Variety, as it is registered as a handler for variety:// links.
Check the option Remember my choice for all links of this type and click Launch Application.

For other browsers the procedure is similar, but you may have to ask Google.
